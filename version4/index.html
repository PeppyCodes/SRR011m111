About

Sri Raja Rajeswari flour & Oil mill is started in the year 1984 by our father R.K.R.C. Nagarajan who is well known industrialist in Udumalpet region for bringing latest technology & innovation in food product manufacturing sector.

Since its inception in 1984,SRR Oil mill has embarked on a journey of manufacturing high quality edible oil & Flour products serving customers all over tamilnadu.
